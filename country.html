My Country: Syria

General information

Syria is a country in Western Asia, in the Levant region of the Eastern Mediterranean. The capital city is Damascus. The official language is Arabic.

Interesting facts

  • Damascus is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.
  • Famous places include Damascus Old City, Aleppo Old City, Palmyra, and Krak des Chevaliers.
  • Traditional food includes kibbeh, tabbouleh, fattoush, shawarma, and muhammara.
  • Common holidays include Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha, and Independence Day (April 17).
